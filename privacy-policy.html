Privacy Policy of Cineras

Last updated: November 13, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cineras, a social network dedicated to organizing your favorite movies and series. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name: Used to identify you within the app.
  • Email: Used for authentication and communication.
  • Date of Birth: Collected to ensure only users aged 18 or above can access Cineras.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used for authentication, personalizing your experience, and displaying ads through Google AdMob and AdSense.

4. Sharing of Information

Cineras does not share your personal information with third parties, except for essential services like Firebase for data storage and Google AdMob for ads.

5. Data Security

We use encryption to protect your data, which is securely stored in Firebase and accessible only by authenticated users.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at suporte@cineras.com.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at suporte@cineras.com.
